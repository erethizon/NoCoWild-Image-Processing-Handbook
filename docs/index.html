North Country Wild Image Processing and Uploading Handbook

Erika L. Barthelmess

2021-11-12

Preface

This book is designed to guide students in the Barthelmess research lab, affectionally known as the SLU Mammal Crew, in the process of processing game camera images for upload to the zooniverse platform. It is meant as a follow along guide that lives on the web.
